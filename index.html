Yo Yo Ma

I wrote this simple Swift app to remind users to text their mom. It utilizes a Firebase backend for data storage and push notifications. Upon opening a notification, the app uses Apple's MessageUI framework to generate a prefilled iMessage to send.

Guided Hunting Tour Location Picker App

I wrote this app purely in HTML, Javascript, and PHP. It allows members of a hunting club to log into a portal to accept the location they were assigned for the next day, or select from the remaining avalible locations where they would like to hunt instead. Utilizes CRON jobs and other server-size code.
